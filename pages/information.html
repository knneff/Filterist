ILTERIST

HOME >

Saving Words to filter

1. Choose a method of how you wanted the words to be filtered; Remove, Censored or Substitute

a. REMOVE - Filtered words will be removed and will leave a blank space on the screen

b. CENSORED - Filtered words will be represented by asterisk (*).

c. SUBSTITUTE - Filtered words will be substituted by the word you set.

2. Type a profanity/foul words you want to filter.

3. Click 'save' to save the word you want to be filtered.

TEST >

Testing if the filter works

1. Type a word or a sentence containing a profanity/foul words you want to test.

2. Click 'check' to know the result.

WORDS >

Library of words and deleting of words

1. Choose a method of censoring

2. Type a word you want to search, then click 'search'

a. To delete a word: click on the 'garbage icon' on the right side of the word, then click 'save' to save

b. To delete multiple words: Check the box of the words you want to delete, click 'delete', then click 'save' to save

c. To delete all the words: Simply click 'delete all', then click 'save' to save

3. Click 'restore defaults' to bring it back to default

SETTINGS >

changing password

1. On the first box: Type the new password you want to set

2. On the second box: Re-type the password you set, the password must match.

3. Click 'reset' to [Save the password][Re-set a new password]
